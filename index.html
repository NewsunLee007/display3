This program is specifically designed as a critical academic English bridge course for students transitioning into the A-Level curriculum. The course adopts a dual-track approach, integrating "Comprehensive Application" with "Oral Application" to holistically enhance students' English proficiency for the challenges of an international curriculum.

本课程专为即将进入国际高中 A-Level 课程体系的学生设计，是一门关键的学术英语衔接课程。课程以双线并进的方式展开，将“综合应用”与“口语应用”相结合，旨在全面提升学生的英语能力以适应国际课程的挑战。

The "Comprehensive Application" track focuses on core academic skills such as academic reading and writing, grammatical structures, listening and note-taking, and foundational research methods, building a solid linguistic base for A-Level subject studies. In parallel, the "Oral Application" track develops students' spoken communication skills, covering key interactive competencies like group discussions, debates, individual presentations, and providing constructive feedback.

“综合应用”部分聚焦于学术读写、语法结构、听力笔记和研究方法等核心学术英语技能，为 A-Level 的学科学习构建坚实的语言底座。与此同时，“口语应用”部分同步培养学生的口语沟通能力，涵盖小组讨论、辩论、个人陈述及建设性反馈等关键互动技巧。

This 14-week course aims to help students achieve a seamless transition to their international high school studies, laying a robust linguistic and academic foundation for success in the A-Level program. Upon completion, students will be equipped to confidently express their ideas in academic discussions, efficiently manage reading and writing assignments, and master the essential learning strategies required for an international academic environment.

本课程为期 14 周，最终目标是帮助学生无缝衔接国际高中学习，为他们在 A-Level 课程中取得成功打下坚实的语言与学术基础。课程结束后，学生将能够在学术讨论中自信地表达观点，高效地完成读写任务，并掌握进入国际课程所必需的学习策略。

Week 1

综合应用

入班诊断与国际课堂适应 (Diagnostic Assessment & Adapting to the International Classroom).

口语应用

口语沟通能力诊断、观点的组织与清晰度.

Week 2

综合应用

句子结构与时态框架 (A-Level阅读底座).

口语应用

50个话题选择、积极倾听与释义转述 (小组).

Week 3

综合应用

名词短语扩展：让句子变“学术” (Noun Phrase Expansion).

口语应用

提问技巧与轮流发言 (小组).

Week 4

综合应用

复合句1 - 从句与连接词 (Complex Sentences 1: Clauses & Conjunctions).

口语应用

Week 5

综合应用

口语应用

关于倾听与发言均衡度的同伴观察.

口语应用

综合应用
